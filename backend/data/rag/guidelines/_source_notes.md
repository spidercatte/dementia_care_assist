# Source Notes

This knowledge base is designed as a hackathon-friendly starting point.

Recommended source categories for future expansion:

- Alzheimer society caregiver communication resources
- Dementia care guidelines
- Occupational therapy best practices
- Nursing protocols
- Caregiver training programs
- Long-term care behavioral support guidance
- Delirium and sudden confusion screening guidance
- Medication safety guidance from pharmacists or clinical teams

## Important Implementation Note

For production use, each guideline should be reviewed by qualified dementia care professionals, nurses, physicians, pharmacists, occupational therapists, or clinical educators.
