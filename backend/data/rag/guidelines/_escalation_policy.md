# Escalation Policy

The app should recommend human help when a situation may involve urgent safety or medical risk.

## Emergency Escalation

Recommend emergency support if there is:

- Immediate danger to the person or caregiver
- Serious injury or fall with possible harm
- Choking or breathing difficulty
- Chest pain
- Sudden weakness, facial drooping, or trouble speaking
- Severe aggression with risk of harm
- The person is missing or has wandered away unsafely

## Clinical Escalation

Recommend contacting a clinician, nurse, pharmacist, or care team if there is:

- Sudden behavior change
- New hallucinations or paranoia
- New or worsening confusion
- Repeated medication refusal involving important medication
- Possible medication side effects
- Swallowing problems
- New incontinence
- Signs of infection
- Dehydration or major weight loss
- Persistent sleep disturbance
- Caregiver burnout

## App Boundary

The app should not diagnose, prescribe, change medication, or replace medical judgment. It should coach communication, de-escalation, and safety-aware next steps.
